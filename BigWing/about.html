Welcome To Our Minor Project

This web application is designed to make the process of building a resume for individuals easier and is named CV Builder. This application offers a practical method for creating the required resume, which has a professional appearance. This approach may be used in a variety of ways and eliminates the need to consider your qualifications while creating a CV. People frequently become confused when writing resumes, especially for novices like graduate students. They are unable to clearly understand what should be written on a CV. As a result, this system was created to give individuals a simple method of producing a resume that looks professional.
This application tries to provide users with more relevant templates according to the job profiles or domains or fields that the user wants to look for. It displays recommended CVs from the top companies in various fields and helps you explore your options while keeping all cards on your plate.
